# Clinical Case Data Sources

## 1. Introduction

Clinical cases in the Virtual AI Patient project must be grounded in real medical data.

LLM-generated content alone is not sufficient due to risks of hallucination and inconsistency.

---

## 2. Primary Sources

### Russian Ministry of Health materials

**What:**
Situational tasks used for medical accreditation.

**Why:**

* Standardized structure
* Aligned with OSCE format
* Include validated “gold standard” answers

---

### Clinical guidelines (Minzdrav)

**What:**
Official diagnostic and treatment recommendations.

**Used for:**

* final diagnosis
* expected investigations
* management plans

---

## 3. Secondary Sources

### Scientific journals

**What:**
Clinical case reports and reviews.

**Why:**

* Provide real-world variability
* Useful for complex and rare conditions

---

### Educational platforms

**Examples:**
OSCE-style resources, clinical teaching websites.

**Why:**

* Structured cases
* Patient-centered narratives

---

## 4. Role of LLM

The LLM is not a source of medical truth.

It is used only for:

* transforming structured data into patient dialogue
* simulating realistic communication

**Risks of using LLM as source:**

* hallucinated facts
* inconsistent cases
* deviation from clinical guidelines

---

## 5. Data Quality Principles

All cases must follow:

* evidence-based medicine
* consistency with guidelines
* reproducibility
* clear grounding in source material

---

## 6. Summary

Reliable clinical simulation requires high-quality source data.
LLM is used as an interface, not as the source of truth.
